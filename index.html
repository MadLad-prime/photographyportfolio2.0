About Me & My Process

Diving deeper, my journey into photography started over 12 years ago, driven by a fascination with Austria's dynamic landscapes and life, naturally leading to a specialization in Wildlife, Sport, and Nature. My core philosophy centers on capturing authentic moments with technical proficiency and deep respect for the subject, aiming always for sharp, impactful results. To achieve this, I utilize professional-grade camera equipment chosen for performance and durability, including high-resolution full-frame bodies and a versatile range of lenses from wide-angles for expansive scenes to powerful telephotos essential for distant wildlife and isolating sporting action.

My process is methodical, beginning with meticulous planning – researching locations, understanding animal behaviour or event dynamics, and anticipating optimal light. Fieldwork then requires considerable patience, adaptability, and readiness to seize fleeting opportunities, often in challenging conditions. Finally, careful post-processing is employed to enhance the image's natural impact, ensuring the final photograph authentically represents the moment captured while meeting high technical standards.
